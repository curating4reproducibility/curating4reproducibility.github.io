CURE at IDCC17

Posted January 26, 2017 by Limor Peer and Florio Arguillas ‐ 1 min read

Limor Peer and Florio Arguillas will be at the 12th International Digital Curation Conference in Edinburgh (February 20-23, 2017) to present a poster titled, The Curating for Reproducibility (CURE) Consortium, which describes CURE mission and goals.

Stop by and learn more about how the founding members promote data quality review in their data curation workflows.
